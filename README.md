# ⏳ FinalTick

Welcome to **FinalTick** — the most delightful way to count down to your favorite moments! 🎉

From surprise birthdays to epic graduations, from ringing in the New Year to celebrating anniversaries — **FinalTick** makes sure you’re counting down in style! 🥳

### 🌐 **Live at:** 
[![Live Site](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live--Demo-FinalTick-brightgreen?style=for-the-badge&logo=vercel)](https://finaltick.vercel.app/)

---

## 🎨 Features That Make Time Fly

- 🎂 **4 Gorgeous Templates**
  - Birthday 🎉
  - Anniversary 💖
  - Graduation 🎓
  - New Year 🎆

- 🔗 **Custom Countdown Links**
  - Personalize your URL and make every moment memorable (e.g., `finaltick.vercel.app/your-special-day`)

- 📨 **Email Reminders**
  - Forget forgetting! Get notified when the clock strikes zero ⏰

- 🖼️ **Add Images or Videos**
  - Upload a photo or a short clip to make your countdown pop!

- 🌍 **Public or Private Mode**
  - Share with the world or keep it between you and your closest pals.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1. Choose a template 🧩
2. Set the date & time 📅
3. Customize the title, message, and media ✍️
4. Generate a unique countdown page 🔗
5. Share, celebrate, and repeat! 🥂
